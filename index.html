Guiding patients through the clinical environment with Augmented Reality

1

Pattern Recognition

Using Vuforia, NavigAR captures unique machine-readable codes placed throughout the clinical environment and interprets them to provide valuable information for patients and family.

2

Active Medical Profile

The information available to patients is often incomplete and out of date. Using NavigAR technology, patients may retrieve a complete real-time summary of their treatment situation and vital signs simply by viewing the patient ID band through augmented reality.

3

Augmented Reality Nav Guide

NavigAR enables patients to navigate the immense structures of continually expanding hospitals through display of virtual pathways, guiding patients directly from their current location to the intended destination without any need for assistance.
